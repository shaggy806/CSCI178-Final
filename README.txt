Our Game: Green Alien Gauntlet

Green Alien Gauntlet is a platform fighting game in which you play as two green aliens duking it out in space, attempting to knock each other off the screen. There are three levels to fight on, and who ever is the one left remaining is the winner. Player 1, called Glorp, can run, jump, and dash using the WASD keys. Player 2, called Gleep, can run, jump, and kick using the arrow keys.
